AI Axis

View at aiaxis.net

Why I built it: My team's AI costs were skyrocketing as we were building AI systems and agents. We had increasing hesitation to launch AI products because of the poor visibility into where costs were coming from and how to manage them. I built AI Axis to help monitor AI costs and identify opportunities to lower them.

How I built it: The system retrieves daily usage data from AI APIs (spend, tokens, requests) broken down by model and provider (OpenAI or Anthropic). I made a public version of this tool, with plans to launch on Product Hunt.

SEO Product Managers career site

View at seoproductmanagers.com

Why I built it: My career started in SEO, and I made the transition into product. A number of my former colleagues noticed this transition and asked about how they can also get into product. I found it would be most helpful to create a job site that provides relevant job postings and AI-assisted resume and cover letter recommendations.

How I built it: The site fetches job postings from Google Jobs using SERP API. The resume builder reads the job postings, parses an uploaded resume, and provides actionable feedback through a chat interface.

SERP API AI resume builder Google Jobs

Corporate slop bowl personality quiz

View at corporateslopbowl.com

Why I built it: For fun! I enjoy the internet lore of corporate slop bowls. Don't get me wrong, I love Chipotle and the rest, but there's something to be said about how fast casual chains have gone too far into the corporate realm.

How I built it: The site is a personality quiz that matches your answers with a corporate slop bowl archetype (Chipotle, Cava, Sweet Green, etc.). It uses pre-prompted AI models to generate a unique response for every user.
