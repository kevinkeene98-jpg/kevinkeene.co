SEO Product Managers career site

Why I built it: My career started in SEO, and I made the transition into product. A number of my SEO colleagues were also interested in product management and were curious about how to make the transition. This eventually led me to build a niche job board site for those with an SEO background looking to break in or level up in product management.

How I built it: The site fetches job postings from Google Jobs using SERP API. The resume builder reads the job postings, parses an uploaded resume, and provides AI-assisted feedback through a chat interface.

Corporate slop bowl personality quiz

Why I built it: For fun! The internet lore of corporate slop bowls is hilarious. Don't get me wrong, I love Chipotle and the rest, but there's something to be said about how fast casual chains have gone too far into the corporate realm.

How I built it: The site is a personality quiz that matches your answers with a corporate slop bowl archetype (Chipotle, Cava, Sweet Green, etc.). It uses AI prompts fed with user inputs to generate a unique response for every user.
